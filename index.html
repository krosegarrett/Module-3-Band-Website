Dermot Kennedy

Irish Singer Breathes New Life into Music

Dermot Kennedy, a young artist from Dublin, has recently topped charts in the US with his beautiful and haunting lyrics. In a much different style than is typically heard on the radio, Kennedy brings together elements of R&B, Hip-Hop, and acoustics into a blend that is distinctly his own. This can be heard clearly in the video above of the song "All My Friends".
